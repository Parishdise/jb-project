About Julie Bird


Julie Bird is a professional photographer based in Jackson, Tennessee. She has been running her own photography business, Julie Bird Photography, successfully from her own home since 2012.





Julie specializes in family, senior, baby, and wedding photography. She takes wonderful portraits with perfect lighting and angles. Her work often utilizes creative, outdoor settings.
